☕ Monday Coffee Sales Analysis & Expansion Strategy


📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales data of Monday Coffee, an online coffee-selling company operating since January 2023.

The main objective is to identify the top 3 cities in India for opening new offline coffee shop locations based on:

Consumer demand

Sales performance

Market potential


🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to:

Analyze sales trends and customer behavior to recommend the best cities for expanding Monday Coffee’s physical presence.


❓ Key Business Questions

This analysis answers the following questions:

1. Coffee Consumers Count -> Estimated coffee consumers in each city (assuming 25% of population consumes coffee)

2. Total Revenue Analysis -> Total revenue generated in the last quarter of 2023

3. Product Sales Performance -> Units sold for each coffee product

4. Average Sales per City -> Average sales per customer in each city

5. City-wise Market Overview -> Population vs estimated coffee consumers

6. Top Products by City -> Top 3 best-selling products in each city

7. Customer Segmentation -> Unique customers per city

8. Sales vs Rent Analysis -> Average sales per customer vs average rent per customer

9. Monthly Growth Trends -> Month-over-month sales growth rate

10. Market Potential Analysis

Identify top cities based on:

-> Total sales

-> Total rent

-> Total customers

-> Estimated coffee consumers


🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

SQL (Data Analysis)

Data Cleaning & Aggregation Techniques

📊 Key Insights

Certain cities show high revenue with low operational cost (rent)

Some cities have large consumer bases but moderate conversion

Product preferences vary significantly by location

Monthly trends indicate steady growth in demand

🏆 Final Recommendations

Based on data-driven analysis, the top 3 cities for expansion are:

🥇 Pune

->Lowest average rent per customer

->Highest total revenue

->Strong average sales per customer

🥈 Delhi

->Highest estimated coffee consumers (~7.7 million)

->High total customer count (68)

->Affordable rent per customer (~₹330)

🥉 Jaipur

->Highest number of customers (69)

->Very low rent per customer (~₹156)

->Strong average sales per customer (~₹11.6K)
